SKI HUNT
GAME PROGRAM INTRUCTIONS

1.   Game reset - HUNTER on the top of screen, Controlling the joystick you
     can direct him to the right, left, down but can not go back. The figure
     on the top shows scores.

2.   Game select - 1 to 16 indicate speed. Step 1 is very slow but it gets
     faster and faster up to step 16.

3.   The scores get from follow:

     HUNTER pass every obstacle . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 10 pts

     When scores up to 300 pts, HUNTER reach to wide-open space for hunting
     by pressing button

     HUNTER hits an animal at the lower edge  . . . . . . . . . . . 25 pts
     HUNTER hits an animal at the middle edge . . . . . . . . . . . 50 pts
     HUNTER hits an animal at the top edge  . . . . . . . . . . . .100 pts

4.   Each game has three runs. Every time score 1000 pts you get another run.
