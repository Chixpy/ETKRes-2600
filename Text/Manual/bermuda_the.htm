THE BERMUDA

Game Description
-------------------------
An enemy has secretly occupied the Bermuda Islands protected by his fuel
tanks, battleships, propellers, missiles and space arsenals. You are a com-
mandant to command an exquisite battlewagon with unlimited bombs. Your
mission is to stop all enemy dangerous attacks by launching bombs. You have
three perfect battlewagons. Just when one is unfortunately destroyed by the
enemy, another is going out to execute the order in place of the previous
one. In the procession, there are a few icebergs to obstruct your battle-
wagon.


[SCREEN SHOT SHOWING ALL ELEMENTS OF PLAY. THE "PROPELLER" AND YOUR "BATTLE-
WAGON" BOTH LOOK & SOUND LIKE PROP DRIVEN AIRCRAFT. THE "ARSENAL" LOOKS LIKE
A LARGE "+". YOUR SCORE IS BOTTOM-RIGHT, AND FUEL LEVEL IS AT BOTTOM-CENTRE]


Preparation Steps
-------------------------
Step 1: Check to be sure that the Video Computer System is connected to
the TV, the power supply is plugged in, and a controller is plugged
into the left jack at the rear of the Video Computer System.
Step 2: Insert a game Cartridge and then turn on the switch. (Caution:
the switch should be turned off at all times when there is no
cartridge in the console.)
Step 3: Select the game with the Game Select Switch and start the game by
switching the Game Reset Switch.


Using the Controller
-------------------------
As you can control the joystick up, down, right, and left, your space-ship
moves up, down, right and left. Press the red button and the missiles are
launched.


Game Variations
-------------------------
Game 1: 1 player

Game 2: 2 players


Scoring
-------------------------
Battleship: 30

Propeller: 60

Fuel Tank: 80

Missile: 100

Arsenal: 500
