SS-008

THE YEAR 1999

Game Description
-------------------------
The enemy commands a contingent of space creatures to break down our base
which keeps a secret illustration of the star Venus. We have six anti-
spacecreature tanks with unlimited missiles to attack the enemy's armed corp.
One of the six is active, and the others are reserve. Our mission is to
shoot them, but we should dodge their missiles quickly. The speed of the
enemy creatures on the screen is faster than that on the previous, and the
scores we get by shooting one creature is increased by 10 points from the
previous screen's points.


[SCREEN SHOT SHOWING SCORE AT TOP, SPACE CREATURES & TANK]


Preparation Steps
-------------------------
Step 1: Check to be sure that the Video Computer System is connected to
the TV, the power supply is plugged in, and a controller is plugged
into the left jack at the rear of the Video Computer System.
Step 2: Insert a game Cartridge and then turn on the switch. (Caution:
the switch should be turned off at all times when there is no
cartridge in the console.)
Step 3: Select the game with the Game Select Switch and start the game by
switching the Game Reset Switch.


Using the Controller
-------------------------
As you control the joystick right and left, your tank moves right and left.
Press the red button and the missiles are launched. To start the game, push
the joystick up and the tank appears.


Scoring
-------------------------
Enemy's Reserve Corp: 3 points

Space Creature: 10 points


Game Variations
-------------------------
One player, three games.
