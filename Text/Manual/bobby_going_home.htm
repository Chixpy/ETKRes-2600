Bobby is going home

© Puzzy/Bit Corp.

1. Preparing:
Following manufacturer's instruction to hook up your video game system.
Turn power on and press RESET switch: the picture appears on the screen.
Guide little Bobby to go forward or backward by pulling your joystick to right or left.
Push red-button on Joystick Controller to jump Bobby over the obstacles he confronts.

2. Progressing:
This game is going on by 256 screens.
Little Bobby must walk through 7 scenes before he arrives his home. Here are the hindrances on Bobby's way:
    Scene 1. Rock, broken bridge
    Scene 2. Ponds, hawk, fountain
    Scene 3. Racing chickens, butterlfy
    Scene 4. Rose bush, ugly bat, tricky holes
    Scene 5. Magic bridge, fence
    Scene 6. Traps, spring of water
    Sence 7. Rolling stones, hawk
These hindrances will appear at random with various colors in different scenes.
    Scene 8. Little Bobby arrives his home.
After the lovely song "HOME, SWEET HOME" for him, little Bobby starts his journey again.

3. Scoring:
At the start, you own 3,000 points which will have added as follows while you progress through the game:

Walk through a scene 1000 points
Bobby fails and starts again 1000 points for encouragement

If Bobby failed 5 times, the game would be over.
Yet, on the way don't hesitate for long: the points are decressing progressively by 10 points per second.
